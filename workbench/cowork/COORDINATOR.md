## Rules

- Keep rows brief.
- Update active work before starting and after handing off.
- Remove stale rows when work is complete and checkpointed.
- Put durable completion details in checkpoints, not this board.
- Treat `workbench/blueprint/` as human-owned product truth. AI agents may improve wording, structure, and traceability,
  but blueprint concepts must originate from humans and final blueprint docs must be reviewed by humans.
- When cowork output proposes a blueprint change without enough human source material, mark the unknowns as `TBD` and
  ask for human input or review instead of inventing durable product truth.

`COORDINATOR.md` is maintained manually and is the coordination source of truth for agents.
