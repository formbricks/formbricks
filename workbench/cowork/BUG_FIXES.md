## Rules

Use bug-fix records for scoped defects. Promote the work to a plan when the fix becomes multi-phase, architecture-changing, migration-heavy, cross-domain, or roadmap-visible.

Validation guidance belongs in `CHECKS.md` and `MANUAL_QA.md`.
